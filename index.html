More About me...

I have always been in love with technology and innovation, and also liked very much to create something out of nothing. I found in the world of programming a perfect match for that.

I'm currently a Master Student in Information Systems and Software Engineering at Técnico Lisboa.
A first year in Lisbon where I've had courses about Software Engineering and Distributed Systems, my major and minor specializations respectively.
I applied for the Erasmus program and went one semester to study in Amsterdam at the Universiteit van Amsterdam (University of Amsterdam).

I am currently working on my Master thesis. The main area of the thesis is Computer Graphics, and have a goal to provide a better way to designers and architects that use generative design to visualize their designs on their PCs.

Tracer

In order to debug an application, it is sometimes useful to know where a given object comes from, or where was it sent to. An object tracer describes the origin of an object and also the places where the object was provided as an argument or returned as a result.

Technologies used:

  • JAVA
  • Javassist API
